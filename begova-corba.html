Begova corba

This is a recipe for famous Bosnian soup.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of chicken meat
  • 200 grams of rooted vegetables (carrot,parsley,celery)
  • 50 grams of flour
  • 50 grams of butter
  • 20 grams of dried okra
  • one egg yolk
  • half of lemon
  • laurel leaf
  • sour cream
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Cook the okra with lemon juice for 20 minutes.Drain the okra and set aside.
  2. Cook the chicken meat with vegetables for 30 minutes.
  3. After cooking remove the chicken meat from the bones and save the soup.
  4. Cut the meat into smaller pieces.
  5. Cut the vegetables into small pieces.
  6. In another pot heat the butter and add the flour to the butter.Add the soup to the butter and fluor. Then cook for 15 minutes.
  7. Add meat,vegetables and okra to the soup.Let it cook for 5 minutes.Add sour cream an yolk in the end.
