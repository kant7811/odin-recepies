Tagarica

Recipe for main course from Bosnia that is cooked in clay pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of beef
  • Six medium sized potatoes
  • Two tomatoes,two peppers,one carrot,one garlic

Directions

  1. Cut the beef into small pieces.
  2. Pour water in the clay pot and add the beef.Let it cook for an hour.
  3. Cut the vegetables and add to the beef in the pot.
  4. Peel the garlic and add to the pot.
  5. Peel the potatoes an cut them longitudinally in four slices.
  6. After an hour add potatoes and let it cook for an half an hour.
