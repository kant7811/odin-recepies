Recipe for desert made from apples and whiped cream.

Ingdredients

  • Eight medium sized apples
  • 200 grams of sugar
  • 80 grams of walnuts
  • one egg white
  • 300 cl of sweet cream
  • one liter of water
  • 20 grams of vanilin sugar

Directions

  1. Peel the apples and remove the middle part while keeping the apples whole.
  2. Pour water in the pot,add 100 grams of sugar and put the apples. Cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the apples and add the remaining sugar and apple peel.
  4. Fill the middle of apples with ground walnuts and sweet cream.
  5. Make cream from the egg white.
  6. Pour the remaining water over the apples and put the cream on top.
